Peru

Peru is a South American country with a rich cultural history dating back to ancient civilizations like the Incas. Its geographic diversity includes the arid coast, the mountainous Andes, and the Amazon rainforest. Lima, the capital, is known for its world-class cuisine. Machu Picchu, the Inca city in the mountains, is an iconic tourist destination. Peru's economy is based on agriculture, mining, and tourism. Its people are welcoming and proud of their ancestral traditions, celebrated in colorful festivals and vibrant rituals.
